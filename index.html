WEX Tracker

Track your habits and improve your productivity!

Things needed to get started:

  • A notebook or journal
  • A pen or pencil
  • A list of habits to track
  • Optional: Calendar or planner

Steps to get started:

  1. Choose habits that matter to you.
  2. Decide how you will track them.
  3. Set specific goals for each habit.
  4. Track daily and review progress.
  5. Adjust goals if needed.
  6. Celebrate your successes!
